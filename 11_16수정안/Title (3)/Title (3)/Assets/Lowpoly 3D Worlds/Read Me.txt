Lowpoly 3D Worlds



Lowpoly Asset Pack with a wide range of models for different types of environments.

Includes example scene.
To change the Camera from First Person to the static ones just turn off the FPSCam and Activate one of the Cameras in the SingleIslandCams folder.



No textures are used.
To change colors just drag a new material on to the model you want to change the color on.
